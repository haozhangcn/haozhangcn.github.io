Few Sample Modulation Identification under High Dynamic Environment

Introduction

Facing the practical application needs of modulation mode identification in highly dynamic environments, we use small-sample deep learning technology as the research background, deeply explore signal characteristics and make full use of artificial intelligence technology, and solve the problem of small-sample modulation mode identification in highly dynamic environments as the starting point. Focus on small-sample modulation recognition based on deep learning, small-sample modulation recognition based on data enhancement, and small-sample modulation recognition based on metric learning, so as to achieve fast and efficient small-sample modulation recognition, with a view to forming perfect identification signal characteristics. The artificial intelligence theories and methods provide important theoretical and key technical support for the practical application of small-sample deep learning in modulation mode identification.

面向高动态环境下调制方式识别的实际应用需求，以小样本深度学习技术为研究背景，深挖信号特性和充分利用人工智能技术，以解决高动态环境下小样本调制方式识别问题为切入点，重点研究基于深度学习的小样本调制方式识别、基于数据增强的小样本调制方式识别和基于度量学习的小样本调制方式识别，从而实现快速高效的小样本调制方式识别，以期形成完善的识别信号特性的人工智能理论与方法，为小样本深度学习在调制方式识别的实际应用提供重要理论和关键技术支撑。
